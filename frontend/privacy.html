Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2024

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • A securely hashed version of your password
  • Usage data (number of recipes generated, last login date)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Recipe Finder service
  • Authenticate your account access
  • Track usage for service improvement
  • Communicate with you about your account if necessary

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is stored securely in our database and is only accessible to authorized system administrators.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Passwords are securely hashed and salted
  • Data is stored in secure AWS infrastructure
  • Access is controlled through authentication tokens

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us and we will remove your data from our systems.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact the site administrator.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Consent

By using our service and creating an account, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this privacy policy.
